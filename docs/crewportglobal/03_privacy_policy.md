## Scope

This policy explains how CrewPortGlobal collects, stores, uses and protects personal and company-related data.

## Definitions

- Personal data: any data relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- Company data: information submitted by shipowners, operators and other business users.
- Platform data: profile data, uploaded documents, contact data, workflow records, complaint records and related metadata.

## Data categories

- identity and contact data;
- seafarer profile and professional data;
- uploaded documents and certificates;
- shipowner and business verification data;
- workflow, communication and complaint records;
- technical usage and security metadata.

## Rules

- Data is collected only for legitimate platform, compliance, security and operational purposes.
- Access to sensitive data should be limited to authorized personnel and approved workflows.
- Data may be shared with shipowners or partners only where this is necessary for the service and permitted by the applicable workflow.
- Retention periods must be defined by policy and operational need.
- Users may request correction, deletion or review of their data subject to legal and compliance constraints.
- Sensitive employment or compliance decisions require human review.

## Operational workflow

1. Data is collected through registration, onboarding, uploads or related workflows.
2. The platform stores the data in controlled systems and logs material actions.
3. Internal review determines what data may be shared, retained, restricted or deleted.
4. Requests concerning privacy are handled through a documented process.

## Responsibility

- Product and operations teams control day-to-day handling of platform data.
- Legal and compliance review define retention, access and escalation rules.
- Users remain responsible for the accuracy of data they provide.
