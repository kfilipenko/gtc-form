## Scope

This policy explains CrewPortGlobal's rule that seafarers must not be charged recruitment, placement or employment-access fees for using the platform to access job opportunities.

## Definitions

- Recruitment fee: any charge connected with finding, processing, arranging or granting access to employment.
- Placement fee: any charge connected with introducing or placing a candidate into a role.
- Optional service: a separate, clearly identified non-placement service that is not required to access opportunities on the platform.

## Rules

- CrewPortGlobal does not charge seafarers recruitment fees.
- CrewPortGlobal does not charge seafarers placement fees.
- CrewPortGlobal does not deduct recruitment-related amounts from wages.
- CrewPortGlobal does not require payment from seafarers as a condition for profile visibility, matching or job consideration.
- Optional paid services, if introduced, must be clearly separated from employment access and must never be mandatory.
- Main commercial fees are charged to shipowners and other B2B clients.
- Partners working with the platform must comply with the same no-fee principle.

## Complaint workflow

1. A seafarer or partner may report a suspected prohibited charge.
2. The complaint is logged and assigned for review.
3. The related workflow may be frozen while the matter is investigated.
4. If a prohibited charge was collected in error, corrective action and refund must be initiated.
5. Repeat or intentional violations trigger partner review or access suspension.

## Responsibility

- Operations team monitors complaints and escalations.
- Compliance owner maintains the policy wording and internal controls.
- Partner managers ensure third-party alignment with this rule.
