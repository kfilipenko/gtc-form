# CrewPortGlobal — For Shipowners

CrewPortGlobal is being prepared as a digital maritime crew data, documentation and matching platform for shipowners, vessel operators, ship managers and crew managers.

## What the platform is designed to support

- structured crew request intake;
- collection of company and vessel context;
- candidate matching support;
- document readiness review;
- verified shortlist preparation;
- audit-friendly workflow records.

## Stage 1 service position

At Stage 1, CrewPortGlobal operates as a technology and workflow platform.

The platform is not presented as a licensed manning agency unless required approvals are confirmed or the relevant workflow is provided through licensed partners.

## What shipowners can expect

1. A structured intake process for ranks, vessel type and timing.
2. Support for identifying relevant candidate profiles.
3. Human-reviewed shortlist preparation before final submission.
4. Clear separation between platform workflow support and shipowner employment obligations.

## Important business rule

CrewPortGlobal does not charge seafarers recruitment or placement fees.

Shipowners and business clients remain responsible for contracts, wages, travel, repatriation, visa support and applicable regulatory compliance.
