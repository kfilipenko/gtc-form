CrewPortGlobal Stage 1 • Documentation and Matching Platform

Maritime crew workflows with a compliance-first spine.

CrewPortGlobal is being developed as a digital maritime crew data, document and matching platform for shipowners, operators and seafarers. Public client documentation is prepared in Markdown as the canonical publishing format, while seafarer onboarding starts through a dedicated acceptance-first route.

CrewPortGlobal does not charge seafarers recruitment or placement fees.

For Shipowners

Structured intake and verified shortlists

Collect crew requests, review candidate readiness and move toward shortlist preparation with documented compliance checkpoints.

For Seafarers

Professional profile and document readiness

Start with onboarding acceptance, prepare core maritime documents and stay inside a no-fee access model.

Governance

AI assistance with human review

Automation may assist with intake and matching support, but final candidate submission requires human review.

Verification

Verified on both sides of the crew workflow

Identity, business and vessel checks are treated as one operational verification surface rather than separate disconnected trust notes.

Seafarers Seafarer Identity & Professional Readiness Identity, certificates, document completeness and professional readiness checks before serious matching activity. Business Business Client KYB Verification of legal entity, representative authority, sanctions context and onboarding legitimacy for business clients. Vessels Vessel Verification Vessel identity, ownership or management context, flag and operational legitimacy checks where the workflow requires them.
