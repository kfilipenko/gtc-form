BP-015 operating cycle

From registration to long-term cooperation.

Start registration
1

Register

Seafarer or employer creates an account and chooses the correct work route.

2

Complete data

Profile, company, vessel and crew request fields are saved as comparable structured data.

3

Verify

Team review checks authority, documents, no-fee consent and readiness blockers.

4

Match

Demand is compared with candidate supply before a shortlist can be created.

5

Present

Only reviewed, employer-safe candidate information is presented to the client.

6

Confirm boarding

Embarkation evidence confirms that the selected seafarer has joined the vessel.

7

Bill service

Actual service and work-period evidence becomes the basis for B2B billing.

8

Repeat cycle

Availability and repeat demand return the seafarer and employer into the next cycle.
