Participant section

Seafarers

This section is for maritime professionals who create a verified work profile, keep documents ready and review suitable vacancies without recruitment fees.

Role in the system The seafarer is the supply-side participant. The platform protects personal account control, document readiness, no-fee access and party review before contract-critical decisions.

Why this page exists

Use this page as the entry point for seafarer work. It explains what the platform expects from a seafarer and where to go next.

Basic order of actions

  1. Register and keep personal account credentials under your own control.
  2. Create or update your maritime profile, rank, experience, availability and documents.
  3. Use job search and reviewed vacancies to find suitable opportunities.
  4. Review proposed contract terms personally before final agreement.
