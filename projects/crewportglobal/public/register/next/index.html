Physical person card

The public registration form creates or confirms the person/user record. It does not create a seafarer profile, company authority, vessel authority or operator access by itself.

Authentication card

Authentication confirms access to the service account and the correctness of contact channels. Email is the first implemented step; phone confirmation follows as the next slice.
