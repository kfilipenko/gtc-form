Seafarer / Specialist authorization card

This card records the person as a maritime professional / workforce resource. It is a request for review, not automatic publication as a candidate.

Buyer / Employer authorization card

This card records a request to act for an employer-side buyer, company, vessel or crew request. Evidence review is required before any authority is granted.

Authorization boundary

Saving these cards does not grant roles, group membership, company visibility, vessel visibility, candidate publication or buyer-side action rights. A team member must review evidence and scoped relationships first.
