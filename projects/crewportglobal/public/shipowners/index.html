Participant section

Shipowners

This section is for shipowners, operators, managers and authorized employer-side users who register vessel context, crew demand and candidate decisions.

Role in the system The shipowner is the demand-side participant. The platform connects vessel and crew-request data with reviewed candidate presentation, contract preparation and controlled agent appointment.

Why this page exists

Use this page as the entry point for shipowner work. It explains how demand-side participants create requests, review candidates and control representation.

Basic order of actions

  1. Register as a physical person and select the shipowner or employer-side capacity.
  2. Add company, vessel and crew-request facts for human review.
  3. Review presented candidates and request contract preparation.
  4. Optionally invite or appoint an agent through an in-system agreement and authority record.
