Authoritative template: CPG-BIZ-132 v1.0 This page is the public English contract package used by the platform offer/acceptance flow. A generated signing instance will fill the variables, parties, scope, authority and commercial status. Russian CPG-BIZ-123 is a convenience translation.

Language priority

The English Shipowner-Agent Agreement Package is the authoritative CrewPortGlobal portal version. Translations are provided for convenience and do not override the English source unless a generated signing instance expressly states another controlling language.

1. Parties

The agreement is concluded between the shipowner or authorized shipowner-side participant as Principal and the selected registered agent or agent company as Agent. The Principal sends the offer inside CrewPortGlobal. The Agent accepts the agreement package inside CrewPortGlobal.

2. Subject

The Agent provides crew-search, candidate presentation, document coordination, interview, joining and request-support services only within the authority and delegated scope recorded by CrewPortGlobal. The seafarer employment agreement remains a separate contract between the seafarer and the shipowner/employer or other authorized employer-side party.

3. Offer, acceptance and platform appointment

Sending the offer does not by itself appoint the Agent. Active management starts only after the Agent accepts the full agreement package, the authority document is recorded where required, the one-active-manager check passes and participant notifications are created.

4. Authority and power of attorney

The Agent acts under Appendix 2 authority document / power of attorney. Without separate authority the Agent may not sign employment agreements, change seafarer wage terms, issue financial guarantees, transfer the Principal account credentials or create a second active manager for the same scope.

5. Commercial terms

The legal framework may be accepted without a fixed service price. Service fee, success fee, SLA penalties or bonuses, billing basis and invoices require a separate Service Order, commercial appendix, request or approved price-basis record. Until then the status remains commercial_terms_pending.

6. Seafarer protection and no-fee boundary

The Agent must not charge seafarers prohibited recruitment or placement fees, retain seafarer documents, mislead candidates about vessel, rank, wage, contract duration, repatriation or family protection, or bypass personal review of contract-critical employment terms.

7. Documents, data and confidentiality

Documents and personal data may be used only for the agreement, crew request and verified platform workflow. Protected documents must remain inside controlled storage and may be shared only under lawful basis, authority evidence and scoped visibility rules.

8. Notifications and governance rights

CrewPortGlobal records material events in the participant governance notification ledger: offer sent, offer accepted, authority recorded, assignment activated, commercial status changed, document prepared, candidate found, contract workspace prepared, replacement, revocation or compliance blocker. The Principal keeps governance rights to view, replace, revoke and request control review.

9. Term, termination and disputes

The agreement remains effective for the selected term or until completion, termination, revocation or replacement under the recorded platform process. Dispute forum, governing law and notice period are selected in the generated instance.

Appendix 2 summary: authority document

The Principal authorizes the Agent to receive and process requests, request candidate documents, communicate with candidates and counterparties, provide reports, coordinate joining logistics, manage the delegated CrewPortGlobal scope and prepare contract workspace records without final SEA signature authority unless separately granted.

Acceptance text

I agree to the Agent Agreement, authority document / power of attorney, mandatory appendices and CrewPortGlobal portal appointment rules. I understand that commercial terms are agreed separately unless fixed in a Service Order, commercial appendix, request or approved price-basis record.

Implementation status

CrewPortGlobal currently supports offer, acceptance, authority record, active assignment and notification ledger records. Future slices will add immutable generated contract snapshots, Service Orders, replacement/revocation, seafarer-side representation and delivery/read state.
