CrewPortGlobal collects and processes data only for legitimate platform, operational, security and compliance purposes.

Data we may process

  • identity and contact details;
  • professional maritime profile data;
  • uploaded certificates and supporting documents;
  • company and onboarding data from shipowners and business users;
  • workflow, communication and complaint records;
  • technical metadata needed for security and service operation.

How the data is used

  • to create and maintain profiles;
  • to support document review and workflow operations;
  • to support shipowner onboarding and service delivery;
  • to protect the platform, users and audit trail;
  • to handle complaints, legal requests and compliance processes.

General principles

  • access to sensitive data should be limited to authorized workflows and personnel;
  • data sharing should be restricted to legitimate service and compliance needs;
  • users may request correction or review of their data subject to applicable rules;
  • human review is required for sensitive decisions.

Detailed retention, deletion and rights procedures will be published in the expanded privacy package.
