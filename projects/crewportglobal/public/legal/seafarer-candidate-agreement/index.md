# CrewPortGlobal — Seafarer Candidate Agreement

This Seafarer Candidate Agreement defines the terms under which a seafarer, maritime professional or prospective candidate may create and maintain a profile on CrewPortGlobal, upload professional documents, participate in document readiness workflows and be considered for matching with crew requests submitted by shipowners, vessel operators, ship managers or authorized maritime employers.

CrewPortGlobal is developed and operated by GTC INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY FZ-LLC as a digital maritime crew data, documentation and matching platform.

At Stage 1, CrewPortGlobal provides digital profile management, document collection, document readiness support, AI-assisted data structuring and candidate matching workflows. CrewPortGlobal does not represent itself as a licensed manning agency, employment agency or seafarer recruitment agency unless and until required approvals are confirmed by competent authorities or relevant services are provided through licensed partners.

## Core no-fee statement

CrewPortGlobal does not charge seafarers recruitment, placement or employment fees.

You do not have to pay CrewPortGlobal to be considered for crew opportunities through the platform.

You do not have to purchase optional services from CrewPortGlobal in order to create a profile, be visible in the matching pool or be considered for a relevant crew request.

Any optional paid services offered to seafarers must be clearly separated from recruitment or placement and must not be a condition for access to employment opportunities.

If anyone asks you to pay a recruitment, placement or employment fee in connection with CrewPortGlobal, you should report it immediately through the Complaint Handling Procedure.

## Definitions

For the purpose of this Agreement:

**Platform** means CrewPortGlobal.com and related systems, databases, forms, chat interfaces, workflows, APIs, documents and services operated by GTC INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY FZ-LLC.

**Candidate** means a seafarer, maritime professional or prospective maritime worker who creates a profile or submits documents through the Platform.

**Seafarer Profile** means the digital professional profile created for a Candidate, including personal details, professional experience, certificates, availability, documents and other submitted information.

**Crew Request** means a request submitted by a shipowner, vessel operator, ship manager, crew manager or maritime employer for one or more crew positions.

**Matching** means the process of comparing Candidate data with Crew Request requirements.

**Shortlist** means a list of Candidates identified as potentially relevant to a Crew Request.

**GTC_USER_ID** means the internal user identifier assigned by the GTC system to a registered person or entity.

**Document Readiness** means the platform status indicating whether documents have been uploaded, reviewed, are complete, expired, missing or require clarification.

**Optional Seafarer Services** means paid services offered to Candidates that are not recruitment, placement or employment services and are not required for job access.

**Recruitment Fee** means any fee, charge or deduction imposed on a seafarer as a condition for access to employment, placement or recruitment.

## Platform role

CrewPortGlobal helps Candidates organize professional maritime information and participate in structured crew matching workflows.

The Platform may:

1. Create a Candidate profile.
2. Assign or connect a GTC_USER_ID.
3. Collect professional and identity information.
4. Collect maritime certificates and documents.
5. Help structure work experience by rank, vessel type and contract history.
6. Support document readiness review.
7. Compare Candidate data with Crew Requests.
8. Prepare Candidate summaries.
9. Allow human-reviewed submission to shipowners or maritime employers.
10. Store workflow history and compliance records.
11. Provide communication and support functions.

Unless expressly stated otherwise, CrewPortGlobal does not employ the Candidate and does not guarantee employment, contracts, salary, joining date, visa, flag-state acceptance, medical approval, travel approval or final selection by any shipowner or employer.
