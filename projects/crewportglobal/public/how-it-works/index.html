CrewPortGlobal is being prepared as a structured workflow platform for maritime hiring support.

For seafarers

  1. Create a professional profile.
  2. Upload core maritime documents.
  3. Keep profile details and availability current.
  4. Receive consideration within a no-fee access model.

For shipowners

  1. Submit crew requests and role requirements.
  2. Share company and vessel information.
  3. Receive candidate matching support and document readiness review.
  4. Proceed through human-reviewed shortlist workflows.

Governance

  • AI may assist with collection, summarization and matching support.
  • Human review is required before final candidate submission.
  • Complaint and escalation procedures are part of the operating model.
