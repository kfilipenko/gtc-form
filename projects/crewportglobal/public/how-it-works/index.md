# CrewPortGlobal — How It Works

CrewPortGlobal is being prepared as a practical maritime jobs and crew workflow platform where useful actions appear before long declarations.

## For seafarers

1. Create a profile and start from the onboarding acceptance route.
2. Add document metadata, contact email and availability.
3. Track completeness and submit the profile for human review.
4. Stay ready for public vacancies as they become available.

## For employers

1. Register company intent and employer context.
2. Post vacancy requirements for rank, vessel type, salary, join date and duration.
3. Review prepared seafarer profiles after human review.
4. Keep communication controlled and based on reviewed information.

## Governance

- AI may assist with collection, summarization and matching support.
- Human review is required before final candidate submission or comparable readiness decisions.
- The public site does not claim live vacancies unless approved vacancy data exists.
- The public site does not imply automatic employment, automatic approval or automatic verification.
